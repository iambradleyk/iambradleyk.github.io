Egg Noodles

(A recipe)

This is a pretty easy recipe and it's probably one of the best parts of the holidays. It's super easy to make and it makes a lot of noodles.

(Insert long schpeel about how much this recipe means to me and how much my family loves it and ya know, all that stuff.)

Ingredient Name Amount Picture of ingredient
Egg 1 Egg
Flour 2 Cups of Flour
Salt 1 tsp of Salt

Directions:

  1. Mix ingredients together
  2. Kneed and fold the mixture until you have solid dough
  3. Wrap it in plastic wrap and let it rest for 1 hour
  4. Come back and take it out of the plastic wrap
  5. Sprinkle some flour on a flat sanitized surface and use a rolling pin to flatten the dough
  6. Use a pizza cutter to cut out noodles
  7. You're done! Just make the noodles like you would any other noodle now. (throw 'em in boiling water)
