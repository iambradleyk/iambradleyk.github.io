River Whyless is an American folk rock rock band from Asheville, North Carolina.

Daniel Shearin, Ryan O'Keefe, Halli Anderson, and Alex McWalters met as students at Appalachian State University. Each moved to Asheville, North Carolina, after graduation. McWalters said that they considered moving to Austin, Nashville, New York, or L.A., but chose Asheville.
